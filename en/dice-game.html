Dice game

Player

Computer

How does the game work?

Click on the start button to throw two dice - one for you and one for the computer. Whoever rolls the higher number wins. If the numbers are the same, there is a draw.

How are the numbers generated?

The dice numbers appear random, but in fact they are not. Computers cannot generate true randomness, but use algorithms to calculate seemingly random numbers. This method is sufficient for games, but is not completely unpredictable.
